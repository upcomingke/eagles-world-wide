About Eagles World Wide

Eagles World Wide Ltd is a leading distributor of premium granite and marble products in Kenya. With years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional quality stone products that enhance the beauty and value of any space.

As certified granite distributors, we offer a wide range of stone options including granite, marble, quartz, and more. Our products are sourced from the finest quarries, ensuring durability, aesthetic appeal, and long-lasting performance.

Our commitment to excellence extends to our customer service, where we provide expert guidance on product selection, installation advice, and after-sales support. Whether you are a homeowner, interior designer, architect, or contractor, we have the perfect stone solutions for your project needs.
